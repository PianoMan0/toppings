About the Sport

Beach Ball Bang is a sport that combines the fun of beach volleyball with the thrill of dodgeball. It is played on steps, which is revolutionary in sports. The game is fast-paced, exciting and suitable for all ages and skill levels. Beach Ball Bang was invented about 6 years ago by Ben and Joel Gallagher and has grown in to a unique and fun sport. Beach Ball Bang is more than just a sport, it is a lifestyle. It promotes fitness, teamwork, friendship and fun. Whether you are looking for a casual game with your friends, or a competitive tournament with prizes, Beach Ball Bang has something for you. Join us today and discover the joy of Beach Ball Bang!

Rules of the Game

The rules are simple.

Bounce the ball up and down the steps.

If you hit the ball into someone's point zone, you get a point.

Besides that, the rules can vary, so choose which rules you like best!
